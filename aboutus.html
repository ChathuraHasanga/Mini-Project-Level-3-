ABOUT US

We are an institution set up to track the whereabouts of foreigners visiting Sri Lanka and their needs. We have deployed our agents in almost every part of Sri Lanka to track tourists visiting different parts of Sri Lanka. They provide us with tourist information daily. According to that information, our role is to compile statistics on the countries visited by tourists, their destinations, their activities in the country, and their needs. We have a large staff for that. The fact that some of the biggest people in the world are associated with our organization is a great investment in our work.
